About MembersPro+

MembersPro+ allows non-profit organizations and businesses with the ability to track multiple organizations and Members, flexible membership dues processing, maintain Donations, enter and maintain Projects and Tasks. The MembersPro+ application also contains a Fund Accounting component that allows organizations to Budget, create and maintain a General Ledger, record Vendor Invoices, and process Checks.
